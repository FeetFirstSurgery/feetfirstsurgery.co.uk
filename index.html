What We Treat

Surgical Services

Natalie specialises exclusively in toenail surgery — providing precise, effective and compassionate care for patients suffering from painful or chronic nail conditions.

Ingrowing Toenails

Partial Nail Avulsion (PNA) — surgical removal of the offending nail edge under local anaesthetic, with optional phenolisation for permanent relief.

Learn more

Total Nail Avulsion

Complete surgical removal of the toenail for severe or recurring conditions where partial removal would not provide a lasting solution.

Learn more

Fungal & Thickened Nails

Surgical management of severely thickened, deformed or fungally infected nails that have not responded to conservative or topical treatment.

Learn more

The Process

How Your Treatment Works

From your first telephone consultation through to full recovery, every step is carefully managed to ensure your comfort, safety and the best possible outcome.

1

Assessment

Natalie carries out a thorough clinical assessment of your nail condition and medical history.

2

Local Anaesthetic

The toe is numbed with a local anaesthetic injection. You will feel no pain during the procedure — only occasional pressure.

3

The Procedure

The problematic nail section (or full nail) is removed with precision. A chemical treatment may be applied for permanent results.

4

Aftercare & Follow-up

Full post-operative instructions provided, plus two dedicated follow-up appointments included in your treatment price.
